Our code does not store email in any way. It is either immediately forwarded to your email address or erased in volatile memory. Information collected by this site is and will be used for no purpose other than to provide this service, and will not be transferred to other parties.

Cookies

This site does not use persistent cookies by default and stores no information on your computer between sessions, unless you request such cookies by clicking the appropriate options.
